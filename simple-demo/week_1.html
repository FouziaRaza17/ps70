Project Idea

This is a preliminary plan!

Device:

Insoles made out of silicone that can sense pressure and the amount of force exerted on the sensors

Details

The device will be able to record the amount of force exerted with each step and give data on the wearer's plantaflexion and dorsiflexion through ankle kinematics.

I plan on using the Teensy 3.2 connecting to electrodes and a Bluetooth module that connects to the wearer's app.
